Mission

EntrustPay is here to make life easier for you by making sure that you spend more time on your product design and worry less about the payment platform. With our blazingly fast set of APIs you are guaranteed to deliver the best experience when it comes to using a payment platform. From our Projection, it's our topmost priority to make atleat a billion dollars in transaction on a monthly basis.

Let's make your Organization and Africa as a whole a new best, why not sign up and be part of a revolution that brings to the world reliable, secured and a new crop of payment processor.

Features

Globally Accepted

The hallmark of a payment platform is its area of coverage. Is it limited to Africa or to Europe and Asia alone ? These are the questions we need to ask ourselves, as suprisingly as it may sound EntrustPay is accepted globally and can be used in any part of the world.

Blazingly Fast

Our platform was built to be as fast as possible, within few clicks payment should be confirmed. This ensures that clients don't end up spending much of their time trying to make payment online because delayed payment defeats the entire process of automating and switching to electronic platform.

Innovative Ideas

Build a seamless and easy to integrate payment experience into all your products, expand to new countries with just a few clicks and track your growth on dashboards, all without hidden charges. We also roll out to you latest updates and new features as they are implemented and stable

Good Documentation

We have a good documentation for our API integration and other services so as to make sure that the whole process of integration, testing and implementation is painless and hastle free.
